Travel to New Orleans!

About New Orleans

New Orleans is the most popular city in Louisiana, a state located in the south of the United States. It mixes multiple cultures, ranging from American to French culture.

Places to Visit

  • French Quarter
  • Cafe Du Monde
  • Mardi Gras (Fat Tuesday) Parades!

Foods to Eat

  • Any seafood
  • Beignets
  • Po-Boys

Is New Orleans a Great Place to Visit

New Orleans is a great place to visit during winter and spring. With an average temperature of 20 degree Celsius, New Orleans is generally a warm place. The most popular event is Mardi Gras and the huge parades that follow with it. Highly recommended to go!
