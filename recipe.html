Pancakes

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 4 people
Ingredients Quantity
Pancake Batter 3 Cups
Milk 2 Cups
Eggs 2 Eggs
Maple Syrup Light Drizzle

Step 1: Pour three cups of pancake batter in a mixing bowl.

Step 2: Crack two eggs into the mixing bowl and fold.

Step 3: Slowly pour two cups of milk into the mixture.

Step 4: Mix fast using a whisk or any mixing utensil.

Step 5: Set a pan on the stove and heat up.

Step 6: Lightly swirl the pan with a small piece of butter.

Step 7: Carefully pour some of the pancake mix on the pan.

Step 8: Fry the pancake until golden brown.

Step 9: Repeat steps 6-8 until batter is gone.

Step 10: Serve on a plate after drizzling some maple syrup (or any topping you like!

Veggie Pizza

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 4-8 people
Ingredients Quantity
Flatbread 4-8 Whole
Salsa (Mild or Spicy) Light Spread
Olives 1 Tin
Onions 1/2
Mozzarella Cheese Grated
Oregano A sprinkle

Step 1: Set your oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.

Step 2: Place the flatbreads on a baking plate (fit as many as possible).

Step 3: Spread a thin layer of salsa on the flatbreads.

Step 4: Add olives and sliced onions (or any veggies you want) on the flatbread.

Step 5: Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on the top (make it extra cheesy).

Step 6: Add a pinch of oregano to taste.

Step 7: Place the flatbreads into the oven and bake until crispy (or however you want).

Step 8: Repeat steps 2-7 until desired amount of pizza slices.

Step 9: Serve with any type of dipping sauce.

Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

  • Time: 5 minutes
  • Serves: 1 or more people
Ingredients Quantity
Bread (White or Whole Wheat) 2 slices
Strawberry Jam Thick Spread
Peanut Butter Thick Spread

Step 1: Take two pieces of bread from the loaf.

Step 2: Take one piece of bread and spread strawberry jam thickly.

Step 3: Take the other piece of bread and spread peanut butter thickly.

Step 4: Place the two breads together, the spread sides meeting.

Step 5: Serve with a cold glass of milk.
