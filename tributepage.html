About Mark Lee:

Mark Lee (Korean Name: Lee Minhyung) (born August 2nd, 1999) is a Canadian-Korean rapper/singer/songwriter who is known for being part of the kpop boy group NCT, a group with unlimited members. He was born in Toronto, Ontario before moving to Vancouver, BC at a very young age. Among the four established units of NCT, Mark debuted in three of them, making him the member in the most units until December 2018, when he graduated from NCT Dream, a subunit you stay in until you turn twenty years old. In August 2019, he was confirmed to join SuperM, a super boy group with his fellow band members and artists from veteran groups, EXO and SHINee, who target the American music industry.

Mark contributes to creating their albums, being credited for writing most songs and his own verses. He was also the unofficial leader of NCT Dream, guiding his younger brothers and leading the fanchant "Yo Dream!". Mark is known for being SM Entertainment's best rapper, changing the public's opinion on their lack of rappers, the demand of rappers grew rapidly as huge kpop groups like BTS and GOT7 have multiple talented rappers. Maybe that is why he debuted four times in under three years, he is that good at what he does.


Timeline of Mark's Career

  • 2012: Mark was accepted into SM Entertainment as a vocalist
  • 2015: Mark joined the cast of the Mickey Mouse Club, dancing with his fellow trainees to senior songs or Disney tracks
  • April 2016: Mark debuted for the first time in NCT U, with the single "The 7th Sense"
  • July 2016: Mark debuted for the second time in NCT 127, with the EP "NCT #127" with the track "Firetruck"
  • August 2016: Mark debuted for the third time in NCT Dream, with the single "Chewing Gum"
  • February 2017: Mark received his first music show win with NCT Dream with their single album "My First" with the track "My First and Last"
  • July 2017: Mark received his first music show win with NCT 127 with their EP "Cherry Bomb"
  • April 2018: Mark joined his seventeen band members to create NCT 2018, who released a full album called "NCT 2018 Empathy", where Mark participated in most of the tracks
  • December 2018: Mark has officially graduated from NCT Dream, as he would be 20 years old in the new year
  • October 2019: Mark debuted for the fourth time in SuperM, with their self titled EP with the track "Jopping"
  • November 2019: Mark, with NCT 127, performed at the 2019 MTV Europe Music Awards, being the first kpop group to do so
